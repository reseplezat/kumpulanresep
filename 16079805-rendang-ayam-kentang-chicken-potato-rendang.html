Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang perlu dipersiapkan untuk membuat Rendang Ayam Kentang || Chicken Potato Rendang:

  1. 500 gr ayam/500 gr chicken
  2. Garam/Salt for taste
  3. 300 gr kentang baby (saya kentang biasa)/300 gr baby potato
  4. 2 SDM gula Jawa/2 tablespoon of brown sugar
  5. 100 gr kelapa parut sangrai/100 gr roasted grated coconut
  6. Kaldu ayam bubuk/chicken powder for taste
  7. 500 ml santan kental/500 ml thick coconut milk
  8. 200 ml santan encer/200 ml coconut milk (not thick)
  9. 2 lembar daun salam/2 bay leaves
  10. 5 lembar daun jeruk/5 lime leaves
  11. Bahan bumbu halus/Spices Paste :
  12. 10 siung bawang merah/10 shallots
  13. 6 siung bawang putih/6 garlic
  14. 100 gr cabe merah keriting/100 gr chillies
  15. 3 cm kunyit (tambahan dari saya)/3 cm of turmeric (add from me)
  16. 2 cm jahe/2 cm of ginger
  17. 5 butir kemiri/5 candlenuts
  18. 1 batang Sereh/1 lemon grass
  19. 2 ruas lengkuas/2 thumbs of galangal
  20. 1 sdt jinten /1 teaspoon of cumin
  21. 1 SDM ketumbar bubuk/1 tablespoon of coriander powder
  22. 1 potong kecil Kayumanis/1 small piece of cinammon stick
  23. 2 butir Cengkeh/2 cloves

Langkah-langkah untuk membuat Rendang Ayam Kentang || Chicken Potato Rendang

1
Rebus ayam dan kentang hingga setengah matang, buang airnya lalu angkat dan sisihkan. Untuk kentangnya saya hanya potong 4 bagian saja. Karena saya tidak pakai baby potato. Boiled the chicken and potato until half cooked, throw the water. Take out and keep aside. For the potato, as I used a usual potato not a baby potato, so I only cut the potato into 4 pieces, its a large size cutting.
2
Giling halus bumbu dan masak dengan santan, kelapa parut sangrai (tambahan dari saya),daun jeruk dan salam hingga minyak dari bumbu keluar dan airnya menyusut, sambil diaduk. Blend well the spices into the smooth paste (shallots, garlic, ginger,cumin, lemon grass, coriander,cloves, cinammon, turmeric, chillies,etc). Cook and stir the spices with the roasted grated coconut,thick coconut milk and non thick coconut milk until the oil comes out from the spices, and the liquid is shrinking.
3
Tambahkan ayam dan kentang. Aduk hingga ayam dan kentang matang dan bumbu meresap ke dalam ayam. Aduk rata dan angkat. Add the half boiled chicken and potato. Stir well until the chicken and potato cooked well and the spices absorbed into the chicken. Mix well and take out from the heat.
4
Sajikan dengan nasi hangat. Serve with the warm rice.
