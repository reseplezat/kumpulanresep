Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang perlu dipersiapkan untuk membuat Tumis Tahu Brokoli||Broccoli Tofu Stir Fried:

  1. 2 potong tahu, potong-potong/2 tofu, cut into pieces
  2. 1 wortel, kupas, cuci, iris tipis/1 carrot, peel, wash, sliced
  3. 1 bonggol Brokoli, potong-potong/1 lumps of broccoli, cuts
  4. Bumbu - bumbu /Seasoning :
  5. 2 siung bawang putih, iris tipis/2 garlic, sliced
  6. 5 siung bawang merah, iris/5 onion, sliced nicely
  7. 5 buah cabe merah iris serong/5 red Chillies, sliced
  8. 1 SDM saus tiram/1 tablespoon of oyster Sauce
  9. 1 SDM kecap asin/1 tablespoon of salty Soy Sauce
  10. 1 SDM saus Inggris /1 tablespoon of English Sauce
  11. Garam, gula pasir, air secukupnya/Water,salt,sugar for taste

Langkah-langkah untuk membuat Tumis Tahu Brokoli||Broccoli Tofu Stir Fried

1
Potong-potong brokoli, cuci bersih lalu rendam dalam air garam. Biarkan sebentar. Sementara itu, panaskan minyak, goreng tahu yang sudah dipotong-potong hingga kuning keemasan, angkat dan tiriskan. Cuts the broccoli into pieces, wash them and Soak in the salt water. Keep aside for a while. Then cut the tofu into pieces, heat up the oil and fry the tofu until golden yellow. Take out from the oil and drained it.
2
Buang air brokoli, bilas. Panaskan minyak, tumis bawang putih, bawang merah, irisan cabe, hingga matang dan masukkan irisan brokoli dan wortel, aduk rata. Throw the salt water to Soak the broccoli. And rinse the broccoli with the fresh water. Heat up the oil, and stir fry the garlic, onion, chillies until the aroma comes out and cooked. Then add the carrot and the broccolis. Stir nicely.
3
Tambahkan garam, gula pasir, air, saus tiram, kecap asin, dan saus Inggris. Aduk rata. Biarkan sebentar hingga brokoli dan wortel matang. Tambahkan tahu. Aduk rata dan angkat. Add some salt,water, sugar, oyster Sauce, English Sauce and salty Soy Sauce, mix well. Cook until the broccolis and carrots are well cooked. Add the tofu. Mix and take out from the heat.
4
Sajikan dengan nasi hangat. Serve with the warm rice.
